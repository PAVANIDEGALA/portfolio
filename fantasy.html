PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project is about a website called Fantasy which is a dream come true for people like me who is interested in art and loves to paint digital and also in traditional ways. I focused more on UI for this project. I always wanted a website completely based on art to learn how to draw, look for inspiration from beginner level to professional level, all kinds of art like illustrations, doodling, oil paintings on a canvas, how to use watercolors, etc...and everything in one website is really a fantasy.


LOGO

I really like the logo of this even though it has no connection with the website because it was me who designed it .




PROBLEM STATEMENT AND SOLUTION

Most of us love to draw but not everyone is a pro or likes the same thing. That's where fantasy comes. Here we can learn what we want or what we are better at and we can choose to learn at our level. If you are beginner or at mid level or even a professional artist, there is still something we don't know. If you are an artist or a designer, you can publish your work, tutorials on how to did it and also view other peoples work. The main thing in this is that we can learn both digital art and traditional art.


FEATURES


Digital art and Traditional art:

Now-a-days digital art is quite popular mostly in designing career and many people are interested in it, so including digital art with traditional would be really helpful to the users.



Photo's and Videos:

When I scroll through pinterest for inspiration I only want to look at images, the videos between the photos always annoyed me. To watch what you only wanna see, we included the option where you can watch only photos or only videos or both together.



Learn from your level:

If you are a beginner, you can search images or tutorials at beginner level and the same goes for intermediate and pro level artists.
